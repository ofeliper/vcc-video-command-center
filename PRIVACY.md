# Privacy Policy

VCC - Video Command Center does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension stores settings locally in the user's browser storage, such as playback speed,
keyboard shortcuts, panel visibility, opacity, and enabled site preferences. These settings
remain on the user's device and are not sent to the developer or to any third party.

VCC does not:

- Track browsing history.
- Record video or audio.
- Download media.
- Extract streams.
- Bypass paywalls, login restrictions, ads, or DRM/content protection.
- Use analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote code.

The extension can access the current tab only after the user clicks the extension button. That
temporary access is used to inject the local VCC controls into the active page and control HTML5
video elements already loaded in the browser.
